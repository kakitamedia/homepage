Profile - Kazutoshi Akita

Kazutoshi Akita is a Ph.D student at the Toyota Technological Institute, Japan (TTI-J), under the supervision of Prof. Norimichi Ukita in Intelligent Infomation Media. He recieved the B.E. and M.E. degrees in TTI-J.

Research Area

Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Object Detection, Super Resolution
